Every k-th period: Payments are constant (= X, X·(1+j), X·(1+j)², …) for groups of k consecutive periods. After each group the payment increases by j%. With m groups the total number of payment periods is k × m.
Special case k=1 → each individual payment increases → use the "Every Next Payment" panel instead.
